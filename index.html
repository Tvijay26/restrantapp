Our Story

A journey of flavor and passion

Founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Laurent, Gourmet Haven began as a small bistro with a big dream - to bring Michelin-star quality dining to a welcoming, neighborhood setting. Over the years, we've grown in size but never lost sight of our original vision.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, treat them with respect, and let their natural flavors shine. We work closely with local farmers, fishermen, and artisans to bring you dishes that are both innovative and rooted in tradition.
